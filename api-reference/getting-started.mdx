---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Learn how to set up and use the GRID API"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for GRID">
    Head over to [alpha.grid.is](https://alpha.grid.is) and create an account in just a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a spreadsheet">
    Once you're logged in, navigate to your [spreadsheets overview](https://alpha.grid.is/spreadsheets), click **Add Spreadsheet**, and upload your file.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/upload-spreadsheet.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the spreadsheet id">
    Once it's been uploaded, go to the spreadsheet's **details page** to get the **spreadsheet id**. You'll need this when you call the API.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/spreadsheet-id.png" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Get an API key">
    Go to your [account settings](https://alpha.grid.is/account/api-key) and copy your **API key**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first API request">
    Use your **API key** and **spreadsheet id** to make your first query. Read the [documentation on the query endpoint](/api-reference/workbooks/query), and then try it out in the [query playground](/api-reference/workbooks/query?playground=open).
  </Step>
</Steps>
